Planets in Space!!


Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, the smallest being Mercury. In English, Mars carries the name of the Roman god of war and is often referred to as the "Red Planet"....



Saturn is the second-largest planet in our solar system, right before Jupiter. It is 95 times more massive than Earth! It is named after the Roman god of Wealth.


Earth is third planet from the Sun and it is where we all live. It has 3 layers - Crust, Mantle and Core. One day on Earth is 24 hours(an earthday) and one year is 365 days


Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It is a complete gas giant. It has a red dot in the centre which is actually a tornado. It is named after the Roman God Jupiter, who is the king of all gods.



Neptune is the eighth planet and the furthest planet from the Sun. It is extremely cold (-210 C!!)and has a dark blue color.


Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and is the second-hottest. Even though it is the closest Venus is hotter as it has a layer of Carbon Dioxide which absorbs heat. Mercury has a dark grey color.
